Other stuff

  • botmaster is a teensy Python lib I wrote that at least tries to make creating twitter bots easier. Basically, after you figure out your OAuth stuff (there's some hopefully-helpful stuff about that process in the docs), you just decorate a function that returns a string or generator, and the rest is easy.
