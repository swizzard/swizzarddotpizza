About Me

My name is Sam Raker. I do stuff with computers, mostly. I work for a company called Neverware, and I'm a Master's student in Computational Linguistics at the City University of New York Graduate Center, although I'm done with all my classes so I'm hardly ever there.

My interests include social media, puns, programming, brogramming, Language/languages (especially dead & artificial ones), minarchy, and wikipedia.

I grew up in Maine, got my undergraduate degree in Washington, D.C., and currently reside in Bushwick, Brooklyn.
