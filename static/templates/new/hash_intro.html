Hash-to-Hash

Hello!

Welcome to Hash-to-Hash! The purpose of this feature is to help me, your gracious webhost, gather data for my thesis.

What I'd like you to do is to look at the following pairs of hashtags and judge whether or not you think the two tags in question refer to the same thing. Answer as many as you feel like—you can stop at any time by closing your browser window or navigating to a different page.

The task is completely anonymous--no personal information will be solicited or stored in any way. If you'd like, you can email me if you'd like more information about how this information will be used, or about my thesis in general.



In order to get the best judgments for this task, I ask you to approach it from the point of view of someone who is looking to find more information about the topic(s) suggested by the hashtags.

NOTE: There is no "skip" button, so just try your best!

Examples

The following tags would be considered co-referential:
  • #debate and #debate2012
  • #ff and #followfriday
  • #raspberrypi and #raspi
  • #Twitter and #twitter
  • #gatsby and #TheGreatGatsby

The following tags would NOT be considered co-referential:
  • #barackobama and #mittromney
  • #ff and #tbt
  • #teambreezy and #chrisbrown
  • #tlot and #tcot
